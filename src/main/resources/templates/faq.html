Frequently Asked Questions

Q:

What is a Payment Gateway?

A:

A payment gateway is an ecommerce service that
processes online payments for online as well as offline
businesses. Payment gateways help accept payments by
transferring key information from their merchant websites to issuing banks, card associations and online wallet players.

Q:

What is the grading system?

A:

Letter grades indicate a student's academic performance. Each letter grade has a numeric value which is used to calculate a GPA, on a scale of 4.0.

Q:

What is a GPA?

A:

Grade Point Average (GPA) is a numeric indicator for a student's academic performance, calculated on a scale of 4.0.
